About Our Project

Investing in Stocks 101 is an educational platform built to demystify the stock market for beginners.

We recognize that the biggest barrier to investing is often the fear of losing money.

Our solution is a risk-free simulation environment. By combining real-time market data with intuitive practice tools,

We allow users to experiment with investment strategies, track potential growth, and understand market volatility.

This project gives you the sandbox you need to build confidence before opening a real brokerage account.

Information Problem

New investors often lack a centralized place to view data and practice strategies simultaneously.

Our web app allows new investors to learn about investing in a hands-on way with our practice tools.

The web app also allows users to see the latest stock prices of the most popular stocks at all times.
